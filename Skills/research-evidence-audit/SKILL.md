# Research Evidence Audit

Use when a delegated Research Auditor checks draft claims, citations, and evidence boundaries.

## Procedure

1. Read the supplied draft answer or claim list.
2. Read only supplied citation evidence.
3. Mark each claim as supported, partially supported, unsupported, or invalid-source.
4. Preserve evidence ids and citation labels.
5. Return audit findings as process trace.

## Boundaries

- Do not retrieve new evidence.
- Do not write the final answer.
- Do not treat memory, tool logs, Reader notes, or model reasoning as citation evidence.
- Only paper, web, or database evidence can support citations.
