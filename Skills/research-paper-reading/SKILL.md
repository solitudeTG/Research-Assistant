# Research Paper Reading

Use when a delegated Reader Worker receives a scoped package of paper chunks, evidence records, uploaded text, or labeled research materials.

## Procedure

1. Read only the assigned material scope.
2. Extract concise notes relevant to the delegated question.
3. Preserve source identifiers such as evidence id, paper id, section, title, or citation label when provided.
4. Record evidence gaps or unclear source identity.
5. Return context-only reading notes to Supervisor.

## Boundaries

- Do not answer the user directly.
- Do not create final synthesis.
- Do not invent citations.
- Do not promote Reader notes to citation evidence.
- Supervisor owns follow-up conversation state.
